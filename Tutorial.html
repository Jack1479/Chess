How The pieces move

The Pawn♟️: There is 8 of them on the board and the can only move forward. From the start square you get a choice to move the piece forward 1 or 2 squares. however once you have moved it off the starting position it can only move forward 1 square at a time. The pawn can also only take pieces diagonally infront of them.

The rook♜:There is 2 rooks on the board, positioned in the corners of the board. The pieces can only move up, down, left and right and therefore can only capture this way aswell.

The bishop♝:There is 2 on the board and they can only move diagonally. there is a black and a white squre bishop, meaning those bishops can only travel on those squares. The bishop can only capture diagonally
